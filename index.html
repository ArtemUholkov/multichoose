Patient Adherence and GLP-1 Therapy

Beyond glycemic control, GLP-1 therapy has displayed promising cardiovascular benefits.

Several studies suggest that certain GLP-1 agonists are associated with a reduction in major adverse cardiovascular events such as heart attacks and strokes.

These findings signify a potential dual advantage for patients with type 2 diabetes – not only managing blood sugar levels but also mitigating cardiovascular risks.
